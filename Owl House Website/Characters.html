Characters

Welcome to the characters page! Here's where you'll find some basic information about some of the characters.

Luz Noceda

Kind, loves adventure, brave, oblivious, and determined. Luz loves her friends and is living her best life on the Boiling Isles. She's also oblivious when it comes to crushes. She has a positive impact on the people and world around her.

Edalyn Clawthorne

Kind, witch criminal, relaxed, and smart. Eda is Luz's mentor. She's relaxed and is good with sarcasm. Her life motto would probably be "It's not illegal if you don't get caught".

King

Loveable, small, and sweet. King is Eda's roommate. He claims to be the King of Demons. He has become close to Luz.

Hooty

Bird tube, is literally the Owl House, kind, and loves to talk. Hooty is literally the Owl House. He loves to talk and can be disturbing and annoying to the other characters.

Lilith Clawthorne

Loves her sister but has alot to make up for, was head of the Emperor's Coven, and formal. Lilith is Eda's older sister. She has alot to make up for and has a long way to go to earn forgiveness. At the end of the day, she still loves her sister and she just wanted what she thought was best for her.

Amity Blight

Smart, used to be a bully, kind, and not good at staying calm around her crush. Amity is the youngest of the Blight children. She used to be a bully but has sided with Luz and her friends. She has a crush on Luz and is not good at hiding it.

Emira & Edric Blight

Twins, typical older siblings, and are troublemakers. Emira and Edric are Amity's older brother and sister. Like all older siblings, they like teasing their younger sibling. They cut class and like to cause trouble.

Willow Park

Sweet, smart, and skilled with plant magic. Willow was originally in the Abominations track at Hexside but was switched to the Plant track after showing off her skill in that area. She was the first friend other than Eda and King that Luz made.

Gus Porter

Smart, very skilled with illusion magic, and adorable. Gus got to skip a couple of grade levels due to his advanced skill in the Illusion track. He is sweet and is part of Luz's friend group. He's very interested in humans and runs the Human Appreciation Society at Hexside.

Principal Bump

Calm and doesn't care much for Belos' laws. Principal Bump is the principal at Hexside. Despite Eda being a wanted criminal, he still allows her to enroll Luz at the school. He is later convinced by Luz to allow students at Hexside to study more than one track, which is likely against Belos' laws.

Emperor Belos

Kinda scary, powerful, and a liar. Emperor Belos claims he can communicate with the Titan, who has been dead for many years before his rule. Belos is extremely powerful. Not much is known about him.

That's it! Now go watch The Owl House. It's a great show with great characters.
